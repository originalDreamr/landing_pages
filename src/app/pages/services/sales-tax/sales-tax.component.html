Sales Tax for Professional Services - No More Uncertainty

If you operate a professional services business, you generally aren’t required to pay sales tax. But there are a few exceptions, if you are a service business but sell products to complement your services, you may be responsible to collect sales tax. Efortles helps you understand the right amount to collect so you can focus on serving customers and increasing profits as well as make filing taxes easy.
